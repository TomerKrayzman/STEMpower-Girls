The Physically Dynamic Lisa McCullough
  • Lisa McCullough, Cornell University

Lisa McCullough is an undergraduate student at Cornell University, class of 2020, studying biology and physics. She hopes to obtain a degree in biological sciences at the end of 4 years and then attend graduate school. Eventually, she hopes to work as a college professor, both conducting research and educating the next generation of brilliant scientists. In her free time, Lisa sings in a chorus and acts in plays and musicals.
